About me

Pretty fly (for a white guy).

I’m a digital designer and front end developer with great knowledge about web standards, information architecture and usability. I’m also up to date with new technologies and social media.

I’m currently employed by byBrick but before that I’ve been a self-employed designer and developer who worked with the entertainment buisness creating websites, merchendise, CDs/ DVDs, identity work and for some of the leading artists and brands in Sweden.

In private

I live in Västerås, Sweden, with my wife Yaty. The last nine years I’ve also been working as a DJ and club promoter together with @tommyisacsson, arranging events all over Sweden. Much of my spare time is devoted to various side projects as I don’t really like just sittin’ around. In general (painting with a wide brush here) I like good coffee, interior design, stuff from Apple and I consider writing code without a deadline = vacation.
