I’m David Paulsson

A typical Swedish guy who’s passionate about interface design, typography, usability and WordPress.

About me

It takes skills to pay the bills.

I’m a digital designer and front end developer with great knowledge about web standards, information architecture and usability. I’m also up to date with new technologies and social media.

I’m currently employed by byBrick but before that I’ve been a self-employed designer and developer who worked with the entertainment buisness creating websites, merchendise, CDs/ DVDs, identity work and for some of the leading artists and brands in Sweden.

In private

I live in Västerås, Sweden, with my wife Yaty. The last ten years I’ve also been working as a DJ and club promoter together with @tommyisacsson, arranging events all over Sweden. Much of my spare time is devoted to various side projects as I don’t really like just sittin’ around. In general (painting with broad strokes) I like good coffee, interior design, to eat well and travel often.
